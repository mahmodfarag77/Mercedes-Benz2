Welcome to the Mercedes-Benz website.

Mercedes-Benz is a luxury brand of cars, trucks, buses and coaches from the Daimler AG company of Germany. The company was once known as Daimler-Benz and is still sometimes called just "Mercedes".

Mercedes-Benz is the world's oldest car maker and the cars they make cost a lot of money. The symbol of Mercedes-Benz is very famous. The three points of the star stand for land, air and sea because Daimler's engines were used not only in cars and trucks but in airplanes and boats.
